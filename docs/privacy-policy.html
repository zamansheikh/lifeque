Privacy Policy for LifeQue

App Name: LifeQue - Islamic & Productivity App

Package Name: com.programmernexus.lifeque

Developer: Programmer Nexus

Last Updated: September 5, 2025

Privacy-First Approach: LifeQue is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

LifeQue stores the following information locally on your device using SQLite database and secure local storage:

  • Prayer Times & Settings: Your location coordinates, preferred calculation methods, and prayer alarm preferences
  • Tasks & Productivity Data: Your personal tasks, deadlines, progress tracking, and study timer sessions
  • Medicine Tracking: Medicine names, dosage schedules, reminders, and adherence data
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, and app configuration

1.2 Location Data

LifeQue uses your location for Islamic prayer time calculations:

  • Purpose: Calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographical location
  • Storage: Location coordinates are stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Your location is never transmitted to external servers
  • Control: You can manually set your location or use GPS for automatic detection
  • Default: If location access is denied, the app uses Dhaka, Bangladesh as default

2. How We Use Your Information

All data processing happens locally on your device for:

  • Prayer Times: Computing accurate prayer schedules and Qibla direction
  • Reminders: Scheduling prayer alarms, task notifications, and medicine reminders
  • Productivity: Tracking study sessions, task progress, and medicine adherence
  • User Experience: Personalizing the app interface and maintaining your preferences

3. Permissions Required

Android Permissions

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION - GPS location for precise prayer time calculations
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION - Network-based location as fallback
  • INTERNET - Required for Google Play Store in-app updates (no personal data transmission)
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS - Displaying prayer, task, and medicine reminders
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM - Precise scheduling of prayer and medicine alarms
  • WAKE_LOCK - Keep device awake for important alarms
  • VIBRATE - Vibration alerts for notifications
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED - Restart alarms after device reboot
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE - Background alarm services

iOS Permissions

  • Location When In Use: Prayer time calculations and Qibla direction
  • Notifications: Prayer, task, and medicine reminders
  • Background Audio: Alarm sounds for prayer and study timer

4. Data Security

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored securely on your device using:

  • SQLite database for structured data
  • Encrypted SharedPreferences for settings
  • No cloud synchronization or external data transmission

5. Third-Party Services

No Third-Party Analytics: LifeQue does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or advertising networks. We do not integrate with:
  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Facebook SDK or Meta tracking
  • Advertising networks or marketing platforms
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media tracking

5.1 App Updates

The app uses Google Play Store's in-app update system:

  • Purpose: Provide seamless app updates through Google Play Store
  • Data Handling: Update checks are handled entirely by Google Play services
  • Privacy: No personal data is accessed or transmitted by our app during updates
  • User Control: Users can choose to install updates immediately or defer them

6. Islamic Calculation Accuracy

For prayer time calculations, LifeQue uses:

  • Calculation Methods: Karachi, ISNA, Muslim World League, and others
  • Local Processing: All calculations are performed on your device
  • No External APIs: Prayer times are computed offline using astronomical algorithms
  • Location-Based: Accurate calculations based on your geographical coordinates

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Data Control: You have full control over your data
  • Local Storage: Data persists only on your device
  • App Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data
  • Manual Deletion: You can delete individual tasks, medicines, or clear all app data
  • Backup: Optional local backup and restore functionality (data never leaves your device)

8. Children's Privacy

LifeQue is suitable for users of all ages. The app:

  • Does not collect personal information from children
  • Contains no inappropriate content
  • Provides educational Islamic content
  • Has no social features or user-generated content
  • Does not require account creation or personal information

9. International Users

LifeQue respects international privacy laws:

  • Global Privacy: Privacy-by-design approach for all users worldwide
